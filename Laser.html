LASER ENGRAVER MACHINE

The project was designed and built to cut and engrave wood using a laser beam.

About Project

The machine consists of several components, including stepper motors, belts, a laser module, a control board, and a power supply. The machine uses a G-code program to control the movement of the laser head and cut or engrave the wood according to the design.

The laser engraver machine is controlled through a software interface that allows the user to upload their design and select the cutting or engraving parameters such as speed, power, and depth. The machine is capable of cutting and engraving a variety of materials, including wood, acrylic, leather, and cardboard.

Overall, the Laser Engraver Machine project was a success, allowing the user to create intricate designs and patterns on wood and other materials with ease. The project could also be expanded upon by adding additional features such as a rotary attachment for engraving cylindrical objects.


Design

The design of the laser engraver project using aluminum bars provides a sturdy and durable structure that can withstand the vibrations caused by the movement of the stepper motors and belts during operation. Aluminum is a lightweight material that is also resistant to rust and corrosion, making it an ideal choice for this project.



Motion Study

The laser engraver machine project is capable of covering an area of 320x200mm with its laser beam. This allows for a relatively large working area, making it versatile enough to handle a wide range of engraving and cutting tasks. The machine's design ensures that the laser beam is accurately directed to the material being engraved or cut, resulting in precise and high-quality output.



Fabrication

The use of aluminum bars also allows for easy assembly and disassembly of the machine. The bars can be easily connected using bolts and nuts, making it easy to adjust and fine-tune the machine as needed.

In addition, the use of aluminum bars provides a professional look to the machine, which is important if it is going to be used for commercial purposes. The material is also easy to clean and maintain, ensuring that the machine will continue to function properly for a long time.
